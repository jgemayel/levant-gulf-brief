Syria

Apr 20, 2026 · Central BankingARHigh impact

CBS strikes Vienna deal with Austrian Nationalbank, reopens Raiffeisen account

Central Bank of Syria Governor Abdulkader Husrieh met in Vienna with Austrian National Bank Governor Martin Kocher to restore financial ties and reactivate cross-border payment systems. Separate talks with Austrian lenders produced an agreement to reopen Syria's account at Raiffeisen Bank International, the first confirmed EU correspondent rail since sanctions were lifted. Translated from SANA.

Apr 19, 2026 · IFI Support

World Bank signs technical assistance pact with CBS for reserves and gold management

Governor Husrieh and the World Bank Group Treasury's partnerships director agreed on targeted support for the CBS Reserve and Gold Management Department, focused on modernising institutional structures and lifting efficiency in asset, reserve, and gold operations. The CBS framed it as the first step of a broader reform path for financial stability.

Syria holds Citibank and IMF Fiscal Affairs talks on government securities and tax reform

On the Spring Meetings margins in Washington, Finance Minister Yisr Barnieh met Citibank representatives on developing Syria's government securities market and signalled Citi's openness to re-entering once conditions allow. Apr 20, 2026 · GovernmentHigh impact

Coordination Framework defers PM pick to Wednesday as April 26 deadline nears

The Shia Coordination Framework, holding roughly 185 of 329 seats, postponed its prime ministerial nomination meeting after an inconclusive tally of 6 votes for incumbent al-Sudani against 4 for Bassem al-Badri. The bloc must submit a candidate by April 26 under the 15-day constitutional window that began with Nizar Amedi's April 11 election as president.

Apr 20, 2026 · Energy

Basra crude routed through Kurdistan pipeline, Ceyhan capacity targeted at 500,000 bpd

Basra crude began loading into the Kurdistan Region pipeline for export via Türkiye's Ceyhan port, lifting total northbound flow toward 330,000 to 340,000 bpd while a second pumping station is built out to scale capacity to about 500,000 bpd. The corridor is now Iraq's primary workaround with the Strait of Hormuz still disrupted.

Apr 14, 2026 · IMF

IMF slashes Iraq 2026 GDP forecast to -6.8%, from +3.6% in January

The April WEO cut Iraq's 2026 growth projection to a 6.8% contraction, a swing of more than 10 percentage points from the January 3.6% growth call, driven by the Iran war, Hormuz disruption, and the collapse of southern exports. March crude revenue fell to about $1.96bn on 18.6m barrels, a two-decade low.

Apr 20, 2026 · EnergyHigh impact

Kuwait Petroleum declares fresh force majeure on crude and refined shipments

KPC notified customers on Friday it cannot meet full contractual volumes even once the Strait of Hormuz reopens, citing the ongoing blockade and damage from Iranian strikes on oil and power infrastructure. Kuwait's oil exports have already been effectively paused for more than five weeks, with storage constraints forcing production cuts.

Apr 17, 2026 · IMF

IMF sees Kuwait GDP contracting 0.6% in 2026 as Gulf public finances deteriorate

The April WEO now has Kuwait shrinking about 0.6% in 2026, with the IMF flagging rising public finance strain across the Gulf from the Iran war. Worst-case staff estimates run to a 14% contraction if the conflict extends through April, with Kuwait's $90.5 fiscal breakeven sitting well above the $57 budget assumption.
