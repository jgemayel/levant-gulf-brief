The Commission forwarded a proposal to the Council to fully reactivate the 1978 cooperation agreement, suspended since 2011. It would restore a political partnership, the trade and economic framework, and financial support, ahead of a high-level political dialogue set for May 11. Follows the EU's May 2025 lifting of economic sanctions.
