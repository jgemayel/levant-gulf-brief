Barnieh puts the 2026 Syrian budget gap at 1.8 billion dollars, narrower than market consensus

Finance Minister Yisr Barnieh told Enab Baladi the headline shortfall in the 2026 general budget is 1.8 billion dollars, with the gap anchored to the three pillars laid out in the April 9 citizens' budget, transparency, fiscal discipline, and productive-sector support. The number comes in tighter than most private projections and frames the upcoming IFI disbursement calendar.

Apr 23, 2026 · Aviation

Kuwait International Airport reopens airspace after nearly two-month closure, Kuwait Airways resumes 17 destinations from April 26

The General Authority of Civil Aviation confirmed Kuwait International Airport reopened airspace on Thursday evening after a precautionary suspension in place since February 28, with Terminals 4 and 5 coming back online on Sunday April 26 for a graded list of destinations. Kuwait Airways will resume operations to 17 global destinations on the same day, with full network restoration set to proceed phase by phase.
