Apr 24, 2026 · IFI

World Bank approves 225 million dollar IDA package for Syria water and health, reaching 4.5 million people

The World Bank Board of Executive Directors approved a 225 million dollar IDA grant on Thursday, splitting 150 million dollars to the Syria Emergency Water Security and Resilient Services Project and 75 million dollars to the Syria Health System Recovery and Strengthening Project. The two operations are the third and fourth Syria projects greenlit inside a year, and the Bank estimates combined coverage at roughly 4.5 million people across priority urban catchments.

Treasury blocks 500 million dollars in physical dollar shipments from Iraq oil revenues at the New York Fed

WSJ-confirmed reporting picked up across regional press places the second halted shipment at roughly 500 million dollars in US banknotes drawn against Iraqi oil revenues parked at the Federal Reserve Bank of New York, the second cargo plane delayed since the war began in late February. Washington has paired the cash hold with paused counterterrorism funding and military training, with the public position that the cash will not move until a militia-distanced government is sworn in.
