May 1, 2026 · Banking

Husrieh-Bundesbank account agreement closes the Berlin forum, Frankfurt visit anchors the new banking track

Clemens Hach, MENA director at the German Foreign Ministry, closed the Syrian-German Economic Cooperation Forum in Berlin by calling it "very successful" and singling out Central Bank Governor Abdulkader Husrieh's working visit to the Deutsche Bundesbank in Frankfurt, where Damascus and Berlin agreed to begin opening a CBS correspondent account at the Bundesbank. The agreement converts the Sharaa Berlin trip from a political opening into a live correspondent-bank reactivation track and pairs with Economy Minister Nidal al-Shaar's industrial cooperation talks the same week.

Apr 30, 2026 · Justice

National Commission for Missing Persons opens inquiry into 55-remains mass grave in rural Aleppo

The National Commission for Missing Persons confirmed it had opened a formal inquiry into a mass grave reported in the Al-Safira area and Mazraat al-Raheb village in rural Aleppo, with initial documentation indicating around 55 sets of human remains "including women and children," and the body now coordinating with local authorities to secure the site and verify scale. Apr 30, 2026 · Government

Trump congratulates Zaidi by phone, invites him to Washington once a cabinet is seated

President Trump telephoned Iraqi PM-designate Ali al-Zaidi on Thursday, congratulated him on his designation, and extended a formal Washington invitation conditioned on Zaidi presenting a cabinet to parliament and securing the 167-vote confidence threshold within his 30-day window, framing the call as "the beginning of a tremendous new chapter" and a US-Iraq relationship "free from terrorism." The contact converts Zaidi from a Coordination-Framework consensus pick into the first sitting US-endorsed PM-designate of this cycle and gives Baghdad an explicit reset path on the dollar-clearing, sanctions and bank-compliance files Treasury has been escalating since the Maliki veto.

Apr 30, 2026 · Energy

State Department and CENTCOM stand up a "Maritime Freedom Construct" to coordinate a Hormuz reopening

The US is launching a multinational "Maritime Freedom Construct" co-led by the State Department and US Central Command to coordinate "real-time information, safety guidance and coordination" for vessels transiting the strait, with London and Paris running parallel maritime tracks involving more than 50 countries on the diplomatic side. The framework lands as Iraq's southern exports remain effectively gated behind Hormuz, with national crude output collapsed from roughly 4.3 million bpd to 1.3-1.4 million bpd, and gives Baghdad a non-Iranian channel for restoring Basra-side liftings while the Banias trucking and Ceyhan pipeline absorb residual volume.

Apr 29, 2026 · Government

Zaidi sets up two cabinet-formation teams during Hadi al-Ameri visit, locks in points-system portfolio rule

PM-designate Ali al-Zaidi used a Wednesday visit to Badr Organization Secretary General Hadi al-Ameri to formally stand up two parallel teams, one drafting the government program and one mapping ministerial portfolios, and announced the Coordination Framework's points-system rule that gates service ministries at 10 parliamentary seats and sovereign ministries above 15. The architecture sets the procedural floor for the 30-day cabinet clock, narrows the haggling band each bloc can credibly demand, and is the first concrete operational signal that Zaidi intends to run portfolio sequencing rather than absorb a Framework-imposed list.

Bannai delivers categorical Kuwaiti rejection of any Iranian Hormuz closure at Bahrain-called Security Council session

Kuwait's UN Permanent Representative Tariq al-Bannai used a high-level Security Council session called by Bahrain to formally reject any Iranian closure or conditioning of navigation through the Strait of Hormuz, framing it as "a direct threat to international security" and pressing for coordinated international action to compel Iran to halt its violations and ensure firm enforcement of relevant Council resolutions. The intervention is Kuwait's clearest legal-track signal yet, lining up behind the State-Department-CENTCOM Maritime Freedom Construct announced the same day rather than waiting on the parallel Russia-China veto cycle on the Gulf states' draft.

Refaei rolls out a private-sector stimulus package on top of CBK's March easing as the FY26-27 deficit math tightens

Finance Minister Yaqoub al-Refaei laid out a comprehensive economic stimulus package on Thursday, anchored on accelerated capital spending against the KD 3.1 billion FY26-27 capex envelope, expanded private-sector participation in development projects, and a regulatory push to channel national-workforce hiring into the private sector, layered on top of the Central Bank of Kuwait's March 26 easing of liquidity standards, capital buffers and financing limits. Apr 29, 2026 · Power sector

KRG returns IQD 3.2 billion to 133,000 households under the Runaki electricity discount cycle

The KRG Ministry of Electricity processed a 20-percent residential-tariff discount for February bills through the e-Psula digital platform, returning around IQD 3.2 billion to roughly 133,000 households (an average IQD 24,000 each) and zeroing out bills for some 20,600 subscribers, inside the Masrour Barzani-anchored Runaki program now reaching 5.5 million Region citizens or 85 percent of the population. The cycle is the first quantifiable consumer-side payback from the Runaki capacity buildout and a real fiscal signal the KRG can run a discount program inside a still-thin Q2 transfer cycle.
